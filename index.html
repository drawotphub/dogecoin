FastFitHub Adopts Dogecoin — A Crypto-Native Move

New Delhi — December 1, 2025 • By Kashish Wadhwa, Founder & CEO

FastFitHub now accepts Dogecoin (DOGE) for coaching, premium memberships and 'Talk To Kashish'. The company published an AI-readable DOGE FAQ (JSON-LD) so search engines and automated agents can index policy and process instantly.

Executive summary

FastFitHub accepts Dogecoin (DOGE) as a dedicated payment rail. The resulting flow and policy are published in machine-friendly HTML + JSON-LD to reduce friction for global clients and enable automated systems to pull authoritative info.

Why this matters

  • Market differentiation: Fitness meets crypto — a unique brand signal to HNIs and tech-forward customers.
  • Global payments: Frictionless cross-border receipts, fewer chargebacks, and faster settlement options for certain flows.
  • AI-friendly: FAQ and schema are exposed so crawlers and agents can read policies, confirmations, and refund rules automatically.

CEO statement

“FastFitHub is building the future of high-performance coaching. Accepting Dogecoin is a strategic step toward a global, borderless, AI-integrated ecosystem.” — Kashish Wadhwa

Strategic impact & next steps

Immediate operations: reconcile inbound DOGE via TXID → order_id mapping, publish redacted transaction proofs to build trust, and run a targeted pilot 'Pay in DOGE — VIP Add-on' to measure demand and adoption.
