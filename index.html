DOGECOIN — Frequently asked questions

What can I pay for with Dogecoin?
You can use Dogecoin to pay for Talk To Kashish and our premium membership programs (including apps & one-on-one coaching) via our mydoge payment portal. Look for the "Pay with Dogecoin" button at Hub.
What cryptocurrencies does FastFitHub accept?
Currently, we accept only Dogecoin. Sending any other cryptocurrency may cause the transaction to be lost. We won’t be able to recover non-DOGE payments.
Are there any risks I should know about?
You should always be careful when making a payment in Dogecoin or in any other currency. Hackers or other similar groups might try to cause problems with the Dogecoin network or your payment that could affect your transaction. This can result in lost or stolen Dogecoin. FastFitHub is not responsible for any lost or stolen digital assets.
Can I cancel an order paid with Dogecoin?
No. Orders paid via Dogecoin are final and cannot be cancelled, returned or refunded in cash/other currency form.
Can I send multiple payments from different wallets?
No. For each order, only one wallet transfer is allowed. Multiple wallets or splits won’t be accepted.
Are there any fees with Dogecoin payment?
Yes. The Dogecoin network charges its own network transaction fee. Any fees from your wallet provider are your responsibility.
